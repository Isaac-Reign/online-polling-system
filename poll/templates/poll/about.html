About This Website

This site is meant to help enhance the abiliy for person to create a poll for people to vote.

This site is free and user will have unlimited access to all the content and even more if they have account.

If you have an issue, at the home page, the top right conner you can see a messages link, click on it to send a message to ICT master, or view other messages

To be able to vote or send messages, you must login if you have account or register if you don't

In the Voters page, all people who have register will be display, and only you and ICT master can see your 'phone number'

ICT master have to give you permission befor you can vote

The ICT master will have to start the vote befor you can vote. ICT master can also end the registration, can also make users (you) view the results or maybe when the voting is over

A user can vote only onece at a 'section'. The 'section' is filtered base on the position of the poll been created. e.g if the ICT master have these sections, Senior prefect, CO, Prep prefect, peoples who have context for these position are base on these 'section', so if a person vote for, Senior prefect, he/she can't vote in the same section again, onelse the next section.
