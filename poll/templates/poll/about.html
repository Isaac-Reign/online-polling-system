About This Website

This website does not diabolist the GH Education System, but for some reason, when there is an election for prefects in school, some level of students may be on vacation or on track.

This website is meant to help enhance the abiliy for one to create a poll for students to vote.

To be able to vote or send a message, you must login if you have account or register if you don't.

ICT master have to give you permission before you can vote, this restriction happens when your name is not found in the school database, and remember do not try to register with random name or twice!

ICT master will have to start the vote before you can vote. This happens when he wants all students to register and give them permissions before they can vote, He can also end the registration.

A user can vote only once at a section. The sections are filtered base on the position of the poll been created. e.g if ICT master have these sections, Senior prefect, CO, Prep prefect, peoples who have context for these position are base on these sections, so if a person vote for, Senior prefect, he/she can't vote in the same section again, unless the next section.

In the Voters page, all registered users will be displayed, with theire names, courses, year and phone number, and only you and ICT master can see your phone number.

If you have an issue in voting, at the home page, the top right conner you can see a messages link, click on it to send a message to ICT master, or view others messages.
